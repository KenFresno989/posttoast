Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

The Short Version

PostToast collects zero data. Nothing. None. Nada.

The extension runs entirely in your browser. No data is sent to any server. No analytics. No tracking. No cookies. No accounts. We don't know who you are, what you read, or what scores you see.

What PostToast Does

PostToast is a Chrome/Edge browser extension that analyzes LinkedIn post content locally in your browser and displays a cringe score. All analysis happens on your device using pattern-matching heuristics. No AI APIs, no cloud processing, no external calls.

Data Collection

PostToast does not collect, store, transmit, or share:

  • Personal information (name, email, IP address)
  • LinkedIn post content or metadata
  • Browsing history or activity
  • Authentication credentials
  • Location data
  • Any other user data whatsoever

Local Storage

PostToast uses chrome.storage.sync solely to store your extension preferences (e.g., whether the extension is enabled or disabled). This data stays in your browser and syncs only through your own browser profile if you have sync enabled.

Permissions

PostToast requests the following permissions:

  • Host permission (linkedin.com): Required to read LinkedIn post content on the page and inject score badges. PostToast only runs on linkedin.com.
  • Storage: Required to save your on/off preference locally.

PostToast does not request permissions for tabs, browsing history, downloads, or any other browser data.

Third-Party Services

PostToast uses no third-party services. No analytics (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no anything). No advertising. No external APIs.

Data Sharing

We do not share data with third parties because we do not have any data to share.

Children's Privacy

PostToast does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we'll update this page. But honestly, our policy is "collect nothing" and that's unlikely to change.
