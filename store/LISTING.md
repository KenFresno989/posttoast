## Detailed Description

🍞 PostToast scores every LinkedIn post on a 0-10 cringe scale — and shows you exactly why.

Ever scroll LinkedIn and think "this is the most performative thing I've ever read"? Now there's a number for that feeling.

HOW IT WORKS
• A score badge appears on every LinkedIn post as you scroll
• Click any badge to see the full breakdown — which signals triggered and why
• Scores range from 0 (Genuine Human) to 10 (Pure Uncut BS)
• Copy the roast to share with friends

WHAT IT DETECTS (39 signals and counting)
• Humble Brags — "I'm SO humbled to announce..."
• Broetry — Single. Sentence. Paragraphs. For. Drama.
• Agreephishing — "Agree? 👇" / "Comment YES below"
• Thought Leader Cosplay — "Here's what nobody tells you..."
• Hustle Porn — "105 hours a week can't be beat!"
• AI Slop — Posts clearly written by ChatGPT
• Toxic Positivity — "Every rejection is a blessing"
• Fabricated Parables — "A homeless man taught me about leadership..."
• Gratitude Theater — Thanking thousands of people for a minor achievement
• Fortune Cookies — Profound-sounding one-liners that say nothing
• Empathy Cosplay — "I tipped my barista $100 and here's what happened"
• ...and 28 more

THE SCORING SCALE
0 — Genuine Human
3 — Getting Warm
5 — Half Bullshit
7 — Full Cringe
8 — Peak LinkedIn
10 — Pure Uncut BS

WHY POSTTOAST?
✓ 100% free — no paywalls, no premium tier, no limits
✓ 100% private — runs entirely in your browser, zero data collected
✓ 100% transparent — see exactly which signals triggered and why
✓ No AI APIs — pure heuristic scoring, instant results
✓ No account required — install and go

Unlike other tools that send your feed to an AI and charge for the privilege, PostToast runs locally and shows its work. No black box scores. You see the receipt.

SHARE THE ROAST
Click "Copy Roast" to share a formatted score breakdown with friends. Every screenshot is an ad. Every group chat forward is a new user.

Built by someone who scrolled one too many "humbled to announce" posts.

🍞 Roasting LinkedIn, one post at a time.
